About Me

The Eye Behind
the Lens

I'm a Vietnamese photographer who believes every moment carries an emotion worth preserving. My lens is drawn to the interplay of light and shadow, the quiet intimacy of couples, the rich tapestry of Vietnamese culture, and the raw beauty of everyday life.

From cinematic portraits bathed in golden hour to traditional cultural sessions at ancient temples, I craft visual stories that transcend time. Each frame is a collaboration between the fleeting moment and the eternal image.
